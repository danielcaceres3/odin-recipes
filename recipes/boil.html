How to Boil Water:

The best boiled water ever!

Show off how to boil water after learning this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Place Water in desired size pot
  2. Place on burner and turn on high. Please be sure correct burner is turned on
  3. While water begins to heat put in a pinch of salt
  4. Place a teaspoon of oil in the water
  5. Watch pot in water patiently
  6. After 10 minutes the water should begin to boil
  7. If water is not getting warm, the stove is broken.
