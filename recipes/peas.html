English Peas

The greatest English peas you have ever eaten

These Sweet English Peas are the perfect side dish to any mircorwavable meal!

Ingredients:

  • Bag of frozen peas
  • Butter
  • Water
  • microwave

Steps:

  1. Place frozen peas in bowl
  2. Add a spoonfull of butter & a 1/4 cup of water
  3. Microwave for 2 minutes or until butter has melted
  4. Let cool off and serve!
