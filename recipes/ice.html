How to make fresh ice!

The freshest ice ever!

Learn how to make the freshest ice to show off to freinds on a hot summer day!

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Ice Mold
  • Freezer

Steps:

  1. Put water in ice mold
  2. Place mold in freezer
  3. Check back in an hour for frozen ice!
  4. If ice is not frozen be sure you placed it freezer and not refigerator
